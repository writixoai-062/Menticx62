## MAIN FEATURE: ADAPTIVE Q&A + DEEP ANALYSIS

### HOW IT WORKS:

#### Step 1: User asks any question
Textarea: "What would you like to know? (Business, Career, Tech, Health, Learning, etc.)"

#### Step 2: AI analyzes question and generates dynamic questions
Based on question category, AI asks 2-4 clarifying questions with options.

CATEGORIES:
- Business/Startup → Type, Budget, Experience, Timeline
- Career/Job → Current role, Target role, Skills, Timeline
- Tech/Code → Language, Experience, Project type, Deadline
- Health/Fitness → Goal, Current status, Time available, Constraints
- Learning/Education → Topic, Current level, Goal, Time per week
- Creative → Type, Style, Purpose, Audience
- Finance/Money → Goal, Current situation, Risk tolerance, Timeline
- Relationship → Situation, Goal, Constraints

#### Step 3: User selects options
Each question has clickable option buttons (not just dropdowns)

#### Step 4: Two action buttons

BUTTON 1: [GET UNIQUE ANSWER]
- Generates personalized, actionable answer based on user's inputs
- NOT generic AI answer — tailored specifically to user's situation
- Includes: 3-5 actionable tips, resources, next steps
- Format: Clean, scannable, professional

BUTTON 2: [DEEP ANALYSIS]
- Generates complete comprehensive output based on category:
  * Business → Complete business plan (Market research, Setup, Marketing, Finance, Timeline)
  * Career → 6-month career roadmap (Skills, Networking, Applications, Interviews)
  * Tech → Complete solution (Architecture, Code, Deployment, Best practices)
  * Health → Complete wellness plan (Diet, Exercise, Habits, Tracking)
  * Learning → Complete curriculum (Roadmap, Resources, Projects, Timeline)
  * Creative → Complete creative strategy (Concept, Execution, Distribution)

#### Step 5: Unique Tips Section
After both answers, show:
💡 PRO TIPS (3 unique, actionable tips that most people miss)
